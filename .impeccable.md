## Design Context

### Users
- Primary users are college students and campus participants exploring KRONOS 2026 events on mobile and desktop.
- Secondary users include visitors checking what to attend and when before deciding participation.
- Core job to be done: quickly discover compelling events, compare pre-events vs main events, and open details without friction.

### Brand Personality
- 3-word personality: cinematic, energetic, premium.
- Desired emotional outcome: anticipation, momentum, and confidence in event quality.
- Tone should feel tech-forward and immersive, while still readable and practical.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Visual direction: cinematic + premium, consistent with the home page dark immersive backdrop and warm signal accents.
- Interaction preference: cleaner cards with richer detail shown on click.
- Motion preference: medium intensity, using purposeful reveal and transition motion without noisy or distracting effects.
- Explicit anti-direction: avoid dated flat layouts and avoid tiny decorative badges.

### Design Principles
1. Keep visual language consistent with home by reusing deep-space backgrounds, warm highlights, and strong typography contrast.
2. Prioritize clarity and scan-ability first, then layer motion and effects as supporting cues.
3. Surface only high-value information in the grid and reveal full details progressively.
4. Ensure responsive behavior is intentional, not compressed desktop layouts.
5. Avoid ornamental micro-elements that do not improve comprehension or actionability.
